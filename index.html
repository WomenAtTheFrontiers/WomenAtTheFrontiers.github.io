The journey so far

We met as members of SoftPower. Brought together at #HackQuantum to solve a problem thought impossible - to end world hunger by 2020. The heart of our idea is community. That's why we're bringing the first community project to London.

Tania Zayets

Tania is currently working closely with women from all walks of life, empowering them in their personal championship. Her vision is to impact the lives of at least 10 million women and children who are living in deprived conditions because their contributions in society are being undervalued. Tania spent ten years in executive roles within digital product management and understands needs to reinvent womens economic and social stance in the Global arena. Tania uses her solid business acumen with creative intelligence to connect the dots, initiating conversations to link advanced technology developments and humanity challenges, for the brighter future of all.

Sophia Wang

Sophia Wang is the Founder of Alpha Education Consulting. AEC provides consulting and advisory services to Asian students to better understand different and diverse cultures, to study in prestigious boarding schools and universities around the World. Sophia has significant and highly respected international experiences in the education sector for almost a decade. She is passionate about Education and Blockchain and is a member of SoftPower, the winning team for the UN Decade of Women Blockchain at London Fintech Week.

Freya Sheer Hardwick

Freya is a computer science researcher in the areas of open-governance and ethical development. She has a particular interest in the uses of blockchain technology and has published several papers in that field. Having gotten involved with SoftPower at London Fintech Week, where they worked to solve the problems of today with the technology of tomorrow, Freya is excited to encourage more women into research to diversify the conversation surrounding where this technology can take us.
